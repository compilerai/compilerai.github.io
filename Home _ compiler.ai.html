WHAT WE DO

We supply tools and components for the development of high-assurance optimized software. We guard your software against errors like integer overflows, memory safety violations,  security and reliability flaws, and much more.

 

OUR TECHNOLOGY

We specialize in software verification tools that automatically generate proofs and/or counterexample traces to validate software code. These tools are based on sophisticated AI-based algorithms within a compiler.  Our compiler generates correctness proofs for the binary executable code, and not just the source code.

​

Our products are based on over a decade of high-quality research published at top-tier venues in Computer Systems areas.
