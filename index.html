CompilerAI's Webpage

CompilerAI is a startup incubated by Sorav Bansal at IIT Delhi.

The primary product being developed by CompilerAI is a Compiler Validator for safety-critical systems, such as those found in aerospace, defence, automobile, and cybersecurity sectors.

CompilerAI is a winner of the IDEX Open Challenge 1.0 and as a part of this grant, CompilerAI is working with the Indian Air Force.

A public demonstration of our compiler validator is under way. Please write to us if you would like to access an early version of our product.
